# ⚙️ Mobile App Setup Guide

Once the app is installed, follow these steps to start driving.

## 1. Bluetooth Pairing
1. Power up your Smart Rover. 
2. Open your phone's **Bluetooth Settings**.
3. Scan for devices and find **HC-05** or **HC-06**.
4. Pair with it (Pin: `1234`).

## 2. App Initial Setup
1. Open the **Smart Rover** app.
2. Complete the **Onboarding** sequence.
3. On the Dashboard, tap the **CONNECT** button in the top status bar.
4. Select your paired **HC-05/06** from the list.
5. The status should change from `Offline` to `Connected`.

## 3. Dashboard Controls
- **Joystick/Buttons**: Use the left side to steer and the right side for forward/backward/stop.
- **Speed Slider**: Adjust the slider (0-9) to change how fast the rover moves.
- **Horn**: Tap the orange button to sound the buzzer.
- **Lights**: Tap the yellow button to toggle rover lights (if implemented).

## 4. Modes
- **Manual**: Direct control via the dashboard.
- **Auto**: Tap the "AUTO" toggle. The rover will now drive itself using the Ultrasonic sensor. Use the **Auto Configuration** page in the side menu to adjust stop distances.

## 5. Navigation
- **Bottom Bar**: Quickly switch between Dashboard, Camera, and Telemetry.
- **Side Drawer**: Access advanced settings, help, and extra configurations.
